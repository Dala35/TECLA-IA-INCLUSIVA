About TECLA

🤖 TECLA is an inclusive AI chatbot designed to support people with autism and communication challenges.

🌟 It provides kind, clear, and supportive answers in a calm environment.

Help & Tips

  • 💡 Say "hello", "who are you", "I'm sad", "what is friendship", and more.
  • 🎤 Press the microphone to talk using your voice (available in some browsers).
  • 📱 Works offline after loading once. Save to home screen for easy access.
